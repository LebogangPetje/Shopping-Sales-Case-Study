# Sales Case Study Analysis

## Overview
This repository analyzes the dataset `Sales Case Study.csv`, containing daily sales information for a single product at a retail store. Each record includes:

- Date: Day of sales  
- Sales: Total sales (Rands)  
- Cost of Sales: Total cost (Rands)  
- Quantity Sold: Units sold  

The goal is to derive insights and metrics to understand sales performance and pricing effects.

---

## Objectives
- Calculate daily sales price per unit and average unit price  
- Compute daily % gross profit and gross profit per unit  
- Analyze promotional periods: price elasticity of demand and product performance  
- Generate additional insights via visualizations, KPIs, and dashboards

---

## Approach
- Clean and prepare data for analysis  
- Create derived metrics and perform calculations  
- Visualize trends and highlight patterns  
- Compare performance during promotional vs. regular periods  

---

## Deliverables
- Metrics tables (unit price, profit margins)  
- Promotional analysis with elasticity insights  
- Charts, reports, or dashboards for decision-making
